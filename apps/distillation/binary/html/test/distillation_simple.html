Binary distillation theory

Assumptions

https://neutrium.net/unit-operations/distillation/mccabe-thiele-plot/

  • The mixture has only two components
  • Both components have an equal heat of vaporisation
  • 100% tray efficiency. Each tray represents an equilibrium stage.


Reflux ratio

The reflux ratio is defined as $$R = \frac{L}{D}$$ where $L$ is the flowrate of liquid that flows from the condenser, back to the column and $D$ is the distillate collected from the column. Higher reflux ratio allow more liquid-vapour contact.
